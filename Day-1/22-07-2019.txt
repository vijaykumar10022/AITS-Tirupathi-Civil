	Compilation process
	.c-->Source code(Human readable format)
	.o-->object file(machine readable format)-Assembler
	.exe-->Executable file/outputfile(human readable format)-Linker

	Windows:
		command promt/window-->Win+R

			2).a.exe

			1).gcc sample.c -o s2
			2).s2.exe
			1).gcc ss1.c -o s1
			2).s1.exe
